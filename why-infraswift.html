Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses does InfraSwift work with?
+
InfraSwift works with enterprises and fast-growing SMBs across Pakistan, spanning industries like banking, healthcare, logistics, retail, and manufacturing. We tailor our solutions to fit your size, budget, and technical maturity — whether you have 50 servers or 5,000.
How quickly can InfraSwift get started on a project?
+
Typically within 5–7 business days of contract signing. We begin with a kickoff call and infrastructure assessment in Week 1, and most clients see their first automation running by Week 3. We don't believe in lengthy discovery phases that deliver nothing tangible.
Will there be any downtime during infrastructure changes?
+
Our standard delivery approach is zero-downtime. We use staged rollouts, blue-green deployment strategies, and maintenance windows scheduled during your lowest-traffic hours. Every migration plan includes a rollback procedure.
What does InfraSwift's 24/7 support actually mean?
+
For all managed services clients, we provide 24/7/365 monitoring and incident response. Critical alerts are responded to within 15 minutes. You get a dedicated WhatsApp support channel and direct access to the engineers who built your infrastructure.
Do you offer project-based or retainer-based engagements?
+
Both. For defined projects (cloud migrations, automation, upgrades), we work on a fixed-scope project basis. For ongoing monitoring and managed services, we offer monthly retainer packages. We can also combine both approaches.
How is InfraSwift different from a traditional IT vendor?
+
Three key differences: (1) Automation-first — we automate repetitive tasks so your team focuses on higher-value work. (2) Outcome-focused — we measure success by your KPIs, not billable hours. (3) Vendor-neutral — we recommend the best technology for your situation, not what earns us the highest commission.
